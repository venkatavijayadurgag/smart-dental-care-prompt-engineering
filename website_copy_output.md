# Service Page Content

## Teeth Cleaning

Professional teeth cleaning helps remove plaque, tartar, and stains that cannot be removed by regular brushing. This treatment improves oral hygiene, prevents gum disease, and keeps your smile fresh and healthy.

---

## Dental Implants

Dental implants are a long-lasting solution for missing teeth. A titanium implant is placed into the jawbone to support an artificial tooth, restoring both appearance and chewing ability.

---

## Pediatric Dentistry

Children require special dental care and attention. Pediatric dentistry focuses on maintaining healthy teeth for children while creating a comfortable and stress-free dental experience.

---

## Smile Makeover

A smile makeover combines cosmetic dental treatments such as whitening, veneers, and alignment correction to improve the overall appearance of your smile.

---

## Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is used to treat infected or damaged tooth pulp. The procedure removes infection, relieves pain, and helps save the natural tooth.

---

## Orthodontic Braces

Braces are used to correct misaligned teeth and bite problems. Orthodontic treatment improves both dental function and the overall appearance of the smile.

---

## Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure that removes stains and discoloration, helping patients achieve a brighter and more confident smile.

---

## Dental Crowns

Dental crowns are protective caps placed over damaged or weak teeth to restore strength, shape, and appearance.

---

## Gum Treatment

Gum treatments help manage gum infections, bleeding gums, and periodontal disease to maintain overall oral health.

---

## Tooth Extraction

When a tooth is severely damaged or infected, extraction may be required to prevent further dental complications.
